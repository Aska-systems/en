Aska

Graph-based open-ended survey framework

Aska is an open-ended survey framework. Respondents can post their own opinions and select the opinions of others that they support. The collected responses constitute a graph, and the responses are classified into groups using graph clustering algorithms.

About

  • This website and the surveys in this website are maintained by Aska project team.

  • Aska project team is composed of researchers as individuals, aiming to further develop the graph-based open-ended survey framework. The primary purpose of each survey is to examine the performance of the survey framework. Aska project team is currently not funded by or conducting surveys on behalf of any organizations.

Technical details

  • Details of the survey framework can be found in the following paper:

    Tatsuro Kawamoto and Takaaki Aoki, "Democratic classification of free-format survey responses with a network-based framework," Nature Machine Intelligence (2019).
