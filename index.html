How Aska works

Aska is a platform for "hosts," who whish to collect opinions from their "users."

Opinion sharing (Users)

The users can post their own opinions, and support ("Like") opinions of others, just like an SNS. (No registration will be requried for users)

Opinion classification (Hosts)

It would be easy to grasp the diversity of opinions as long as your users are not so many. But what if you have hundreds of users? In Aska, a large number of opinions can be classified using machine-learning techniques.

Features

Functions as a survey framework

You can use Aska as a survey framework, in which you can ask open-ended questions.

  • Aska has templates for multiple-choice questions
  • Although we do not have our own web panels, you can naturally use Aska in various services
    • Amazon Mechanical Turk (MTurk)

      • We have an optoin to generate a verification code, which is required for surveys in MTurk

    • Lucid

      • We have a redirect template for Lucid
